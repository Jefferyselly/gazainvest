OUR PRIVACY POLICY

The statement contained in the Terms & Conditions shall serve as an attestation to the agreement of mutual benefit between the Investor and GAZA Investment Limited. Conceived by recognizing the essence of establishing an agreement of investment for promotion and protection of investment at the interest of both parties, proceeding into the investment contract and signing the Memorandum of Understanding means that the Investor has read, understood and agreed to the terms and conditions of the said contract.

Definition

The term ”investment” shall mean the amount of funds committed into the investment contract in accordance with the provision of GAZA Investment Limited in which the investment was made. The term ”returns” shall mean the amount of profit, and sums received on the investment in accordance with the provision of the Contracting Party The term ”investor” shall refer to any person or legal entity, including a corporation, a firm, an association or a partnership making an investment with GAZA Investment Limited.

USE & PROTECTION OF FUNDS

Yes Capital Limited shall put necessary efforts in place to secure the funds provided by the Investor in accordance with the agreement reached between GAZA Investment Limited and the Investor. GAZA Investment Limited shall be using the funds as stated in the agreement— to trade the forex market, dealings in a real estate and procurement of agricultural proceeds and invest in the growth of the company.

REPATRIATION OF INVESTMENT AND RETURNS

GAZA Investment Limited shall refund the Investor plus the profit earned on investment in the agreed form, method, and system of repatriation at the end of the investment plan in accordance to the mode of request made by the Investor. In any case, if the investor decides to terminate the Investment plan and obtain full money prior to the agreed date of repatriation. A non-negotiable 10% penalty fee shall be deducted for breach of contract, inconvenience caused and all interest accrued shall be forfeited. And this can only be processed in special cases.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Both Contracting Parties shall, keep in strict confidence all financial, commercial and technical information belonging to the other party, or which was jointly developed by both parties in preparation of the definitive agreement. Whereas, without prior written consent of the other party, neither party shall at any time disclose any confidential information to any third party, save and except where it is necessary for the Agreement or by court order from a financial regulatory bodies in the country.

ARBITRATION

The Investor , GAZA Investment Limited but both parties shall use reasonable measures to settle any disputes arising from this agreement, amicably through discussions. If the issues cannot be resolved in 14 days, such claims will be resolved by binding arbitration, rather than in court. Each party shall provide their own arbitrator. Both arbitrators shall agree on another (neutral) arbitrator whose decision will be final. The arbitration shall be governed by Nigerian law pursuant to the Arbitration and Conciliation Act Cap A18 Laws of the Federation of Nigerian, 2004, as amended, replaced and re-enacted from time to time

RISK POLICY

GAZA investment Limited guarantees protection and safety of funds, and if in any case, the fund committed into the contract in accordance with the agreement made has received constant returns for 6 months. Any disputes arising in relation to funds shall be strictly settled in accordance with the arbitration terms and conditions.
